Getting Started

Our application uses a simple and straight forward step-by-step approach. We will guide you through in inserting all the necessary values and materials.

Inputting the values is easy. Open a form from "Add a component", then just simply input values and materials. If you dont know some value of a component, dont worry! You can just choose a default material.

After inputting your values, our application does the calculations for you and suggests possible improvements in energy efficiency.

Our custom made measurement system will also provide you with information about how much the improvement will pay you back.
